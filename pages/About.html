About Us

Chateau Chien

Amazing Trainers for Fur Friends

At Chateau Chien, we open our doors to all our furry companions, inviting them to bring along their beloved humans for a transformative experience. Our mission is simple: to empower dogs and their owners alike through tailored training programs designed to foster harmony and happiness in every household.

Whether you're a playful pup just beginning to explore the world with your new family, a young dog eager to learn your first tricks, or a seasoned companion in need of a gentle reminder on manners, Chateau Chien is here for you. Our diverse range of classes ensures that there's something for every dog, regardless of age or skill level.

At the heart of our approach lies the bond between dog and human. Our experienced trainers not only guide your furry friend through obedience training but also empower you, the owner, to become the best possible leader and companion. Through positive reinforcement and patience, we instill confidence in both dogs and their humans, fostering a relationship built on trust and understanding.

Join us for our group classes, where you'll have the opportunity to socialize with fellow canines, observe their progress, and learn alongside them. These sessions provide a supportive environment for dogs to interact, play, and learn from one another, all while their humans receive guidance from our dedicated trainers.

For those who prefer a more personalized approach, we offer private sessions tailored to address specific needs and challenges. Whether your dog requires extra attention or you simply prefer one-on-one instruction, our trainers are here to provide individualized support and guidance every step of the way.

At Chateau Chien, we believe that every dog deserves the chance to thrive, and every owner deserves the tools to nurture that potential. Join us on this journey of growth, learning, and companionship, and together, let's create a harmonious bond that lasts a lifetime.

Founder

Matthew Kim is the visionary founder behind Chateau Chien, a haven for dogs and their owners seeking personalized training solutions. With a passion for canine behavior and a commitment to strengthening the bond between dogs and humans, Matthew embarked on a mission to create a space where every dog could flourish.

Drawing upon his extensive experience in dog training and behavior modification, Matthew Kim established Chateau Chien as a place of learning, growth, and connection. His innovative approach to training emphasizes positive reinforcement and individualized instruction, empowering both dogs and their owners to reach their full potential.

Driven by a deep understanding of canine psychology and a genuine love for animals, Matthew Kim has dedicated himself to fostering harmonious relationships between dogs and their humans. Through his leadership and expertise, Chateau Chien has become a trusted resource for dog owners seeking guidance, support, and companionship on their journey with their beloved pets.

With Matthew Kim at the helm, Chateau Chien continues to serve as a beacon of hope and possibility for dogs and their owners, enriching lives one tail wag at a time.
